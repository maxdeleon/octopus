To Call:
	!volE ticker dayrange
Parameters:
	ticker symbol — ex. USO
	dayrange from now — ex. 250

Displays:
	Realized Volatility
	Volatilistic Elasticity of Returns.

Volatilistic Elasticity of Returns = %ΔP/%ΔV
	“percent change in price over the percent change in volatility”
	Means: to what extent a change in volatility affects a change in price
	Use: helps to adjust limits for massive volatility changes or gapping
